F1 Driving Game

About the Game

Test your driving skills against competitive AI drivers and test your might against the most grueling tracks imaginable in this F1 simulator.

How to Play

Follow these steps to drive your car to victory:

    • Up Arrow:Move up
    • Down Arrow:Move down
    • Left Arrow:Move left
    • Right Arrow:Move right
  1. Click inside the game window to activate controls.
  2. Use the arrow keys (← ↑ ↓ →) to move your car
  3. stay on track and make it to the finish line first.
