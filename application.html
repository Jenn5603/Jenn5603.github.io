Maze Solver Game

About the Game

Test your problem-solving skills with this interactive maze game! Use the arrow keys to navigate through the maze and find your way to the exit.

How to Play

Follow these steps to solve the maze:

    • Up Arrow:Move up
    • Down Arrow:Move down
    • Left Arrow:Move left
    • Right Arrow:Move right
  1. Click inside the game window to activate controls.
  2. Use the arrow keys (← ↑ ↓ →) to move your character.
  3. Find the shortest path to reach the exit.
