F1 Racing Simulator

About the Game

Experience the thrill of high-speed racing with this F1 simulator. Compete against AI opponents and master challenging tracks.

How to Play

Follow these instructions to navigate the game:

  • Up Arrow: Accelerate
  • Down Arrow: Brake/Reverse
  • Left Arrow: Steer Left
  • Right Arrow: Steer Right
  1. Click inside the game window to activate controls.
  2. Stay on track and avoid crashing.
  3. Cross the finish line before your opponents!
